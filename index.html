IP Wallet Registration

People from countries sanctioned by the United States of America must register the IP address of their hot wallets and cryptocurrency wallets via email; otherwise, they will be subject to OFAC sanctions.

  • Iran
  • North Korea
  • Cuba
  • Syria
  • Crimea, Donetsk, Luhansk
  • Venezuela
  • Russia (restricted entities)

A real person should send a message from their personal email address to the email address provided on the page, include the name of their country, and prevent the blockage of their wallet via email.
