IP Wallet Registration

According to OFAC regulations, users from sanctioned countries must register their wallet IP address.

  • Iran
  • North Korea
  • Cuba
  • Syria
  • Crimea, Donetsk, Luhansk
  • Venezuela
  • Russia (restricted entities)

A real person should send a message from their personal email address to the provided email, stating the name of their country and requesting to prevent the blockage of their wallet.
